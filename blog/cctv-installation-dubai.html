2. Understand Dubai's Legal CCTV Regulations

Dubai Municipality and SIRA (Security Industry Regulatory Agency) enforce specific laws for CCTV setup. Avoid installing cameras in private zones, and always hire a SIRA-certified installer to avoid fines and ensure legal coverage.

3. Optimize Camera Placement for Full Coverage

Strategic camera placement is key. Cover entrances, exits, elevators, parking areas, and cash counters. For businesses, monitor server rooms, reception areas, and storage units. Prevent glare by avoiding direct sunlight or bright lights.

4. Use Smart Storage & Remote Viewing Options
