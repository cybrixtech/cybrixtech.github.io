Top 5 CCTV Installation Tips for Dubai Homes & Offices

Published by CyBrix Technologies | July 27, 2025

1. Choose the Right Type of Camera

Bullet cameras are great for outdoor use, while dome cameras are discreet and suited for indoor environments. Consider your location’s needs before picking a type.

2. Understand the Legal Requirements

In Dubai, CCTV installation must comply with local regulations. Always use approved vendors and avoid installing cameras in private or restricted areas.

3. Optimize Camera Placement

Install cameras at entry points, parking lots, server rooms, and hallways. Avoid direct sunlight or glare, and make sure no blind spots are left uncovered.

4. Choose the Right Storage Option

Decide between cloud or local storage based on your internet connectivity, budget, and data sensitivity. Hybrid systems offer both options.

5. Schedule Regular Maintenance

Dust, heat, or power issues can impact performance. Plan quarterly checkups and software updates to ensure your system always works.
